About Me



Hello! My name is Rahil and I graduated from Fort Hays State University with a Bachelor's of Science Degree in Computer Science. I made this website during my last year of college. I would like to show those who would like to learn Japanese, a fun and calm path that integrates both official resources and Japanese media. In doing so, perhaps I can be the motivation that people seek.


Best of luck to anyone on the adventure of studying Japanese! 頑張って!
