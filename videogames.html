Pokémon

The core RPG Pokémon series has to be the best beginner friendly game to supplement Japanese studies as the vocabulary is on the easier side and it is a series that most people have played. Another benefit to this series is that there is little to no Kanji. One misconception that many people have is that there are a lot of loopholes to jump through in order to start playing a Japanese video game. This is incorrect as the Pokémon series is at the very least one of the few that includes Japanese in some of the newer games.


Upon doing some research on YouTube, I came across this channel that walks through Japanese games and explains the vocabulary along with the grammar. This can be incredibly helpful and this YouTuber has a variety of games on his channel.
