My Anime Story

I have listed some anime that are great for beginners as they are either short or use easier vocabulary. Watching an episode every day and trying to figure out what the characters are saying should not be as difficult as some of the more complex anime.

If you would like to watch anime online with both the Japanese and English subtitles with an easy toggle feature, then go to Animelon. If you would like to get the Japanese subtitles for various anime (which will be explained in the tuorials), then go to Kitsuneko.
